We digitize electronic health records. Ship or email us your medical records (prescriptions, test reports, scans). We combine all your medical data into one PDF and return your physical reports.

Your medical data is stored only on ONITO's private server and shared only with you and recipients you give access to. We take data privacy seriously.

In the unfortunate event a user passes away, ONITO anonymizes and uses their medical data for machine learning model training to assist doctors with machine learning aided clinical diagnostics. Email us to opt out of this service. By default, all users are signed up for data sharing after death.
