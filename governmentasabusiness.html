GOVERNMENT AS A BUSINESS


Government produces and sells goods in a free market. It incentivized to produce the best goods for the good of the people. Government profits from certain businesses and funds loss leaders like free healthcare, education, housing for all, national parks, and libraries. Services to build trust in the government.

In this sense, the government is a company. A money-making machine that makes itself more efficient over time through scientific discoveries. The efficiency gains translate to greater profits that are spent for the good of its citizens (or lower prices for goods).
