CREATING AN NPC SOCIETY


I want to test the effects of communication between equivalent NLP models (agents).

My strategy:

1. Conversations between 2 agents are seeded with different inputs and recorded

2. Conversational data is used for retraining agent is differentiated

from client training data with a unique label and different weight

I don’t have any specific reasons to do this. I just want to study the

importance of communication and simulate the emergence of language. I

feel it’ll hold a lot of insight.
