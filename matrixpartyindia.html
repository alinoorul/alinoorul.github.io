Political manifesto

Matrixparty is a political party for India to campaign in Lok Sabha (national) and Vidhan Sabha (state) elections. Matrixparty political manifestos always have only 20 points for readability and government focus.

Matrixparty India Manifesto 2025 by Noorul Ali, party founder and chief

1. Kashmir dispute resolution with referendum

2. Reduce air pollution

3. Decrease government bureaucracy

4. Decrease Indian Administrative Service (IAS) workforce by limiting employment to rural and semi-urban regions

5. Set up for-profit consultancies with ex-IAS employees to aid government projects

6. Set up Internal Affairs (Police) to reduce bribery

7. Limit political representation of uneducated citizens to force education till 12th standard

8. Kickstart innovation economy to fight middle income trap (1)

9. Innovation purchases by government to make Intellectual Property creation and entrepreneurship viable careers

10. Increase defence budget by 20%

11. Increase taxes on income and capital gains above ₹100 crore annually

12. Decriminalize cannabis for medical and recreational use

13. Legalize cannabis for recreational and medical use in Union Territories

14. Tax annual agricultural income over ₹10 crore

15. Encourage research in biotechnology for cancer like CAR-T therapy (2) and mRNA therapy (3), and low-power artificial intelligence using memristors (4)

16. Develop research culture with high-paying jobs

17. Fast-track court cases with undeniable proof in local courts and police stations with ex-IAS as judges

18. Mandatory national civic sense training

19. Enforce civic laws on littering, abuse and harrassment with imprisonment

20. Decrease agricultural employment and encourage large landholder farms to increase exports

Kashmir dispute resolution
On 22 April 2025, Pahalgam terrorist attack occurred in Baisaran Valley, Kashmir. India, Pakistan, China, and Kashmiris have still not resolved the territorial dispute over Kashmir. These sides of the story must be heard: Kashmiris' right to choose, Kashmiri Pandit exodus changing Kashmir's demographics, Pakistan's claim according to religious demographics in 1947, and Chinese control of Aksai Chin. A referendum (plebiscite) is pending since 1947 and must be conducted. India fears this referendum because Kashmiri Pandits may not get votes though they are rightful residents of the regions, and the Muslim demographic may side with Pakistan's claim over the region. This fear is rational. Matrixparty does not want to give up Kashmir. Voting is necessary for lasting peace in Kashmir. Matrixparty will heavily campaign in Kashmir to convince Kashmiris to side with India. India is successful, secular and safe. Pakistan is none of the three and cannot afford to develop Kashmir. Matrixparty is prepared to make concessions to Kashmir like autonomy in state governance and no income tax. Matrixparty plans to achieve lasting peace in this region. We make no guarantees to secure all of Kashmir (including Pakistan-occupied Kashmir and Aksai Chin) for India. The referendum will collect data for Matrixparty on Kashmiri choice to better inform India's Kashmir strategy. The eventual goal is to enforce the will of the Kashmiri people after convincing Kashmir's residents to choose to be part of India. With India, a bright future awaits. Alternatives are Kashmiri independence which requires trade with India for all material goods, or Pakistani Kashmir which is infeasible due to Pakistan's debt-ridden failed economy. India is Kashmir's best bet.

Referendum has 3 choices: India, Pakistan, Azad Kashmir. District-wise voting will be done. Matrixparty will discuss and decide what to do with districts voting for Pakistan or Azad Kashmir. This referendum is primarily for Gilgit-Baltistan, Azad Kashmir, and Aksai Chin regions. India will retain sovereignty of Jammu and Kashmir and Ladakh regions regardless of referendum votes of these 2 regions. This referendum guarantees representation of Kashmiri residents and descendants of 1947 Kashmiris. Matrixparty makes no guarantees to enforce results of the referendum across the region because multiple parties are involved who must agree to a diplomatic resolution. Rest assured, Kashmiri choice will influence Matrixparty policy on Kashmir in favor of Kashmiris.
