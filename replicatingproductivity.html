REPLICATING PRODUCTIVITY


I would like to encode some semblance of machine sentience in the Matrix.

For example, let us say one of Alcor’s clients was the world’s best

neurosurgeons. We replicate them in the Matrix.

Using robotic surgical tools, the digital version of neurosurgeon performs

surgeries. Now rather than interpreting that just as a replicable program, we

interpret it as a somewhat sentient version of that human, but also a

program.

I would like for this to occur in the Matrix. Currently alive people can use

metawork data (how they do work, what work they produce) to train bot

version of themselves in the Matrix.

These programs are in a sense versions of humans, but with UniProGResS

they’re their own beings as well. I am still undecided what to interpret them

as.
