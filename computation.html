COMPUTATION


Computation is control and replicability. It must exist as information in our

universe. A physical thing that is a consequence of our mathematical models

of the universe. Observable through the machines we create within our

mathematical models of the universe.

Information is a quantity I theorize. But I believe it exists in a duality. It may

be present as a consequence of our desire to measure it. Or it may be

present regardless of our measurement.
