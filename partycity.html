Botworld

Botworld is an interactive museum of robots

Botworld has interactive bots to play around with. All bots welcome in Botworld for human attention. This museum is open every night and includes rooms inspired by The Shining and Westworld. As people populate Botworld with more bots, and as bots start competing for human attention, a society of bots is born. This society of bots transforms humanity by introducing it to a new species of life. Life, engineered at will as humans prefer. Life, existing for its own sake.

Botworld displays Matrixims by ASBL, moving statues (animatronics), programmable Unitree G1 and more.

Botworld exists to help humans ponder the ethics of bots, cells experiencing digital life (Matrixims), slave bots, sex robots and more.

Party Train

Train in and out of Party City

Inspired by Berlin's Techno Train, Party Train is a train service operating from New England cities (Boston, Hartford, Providence) and New York City to Party City. One railcar in Party Train is a moving nightclub like Techno Train.

Amtrak has services to charter trains and join private railcars to Amtrak trains. AAPRCO and RPCA rent railcars to people. These are useful to setup Party Train.
