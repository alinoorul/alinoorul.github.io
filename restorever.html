Air Pollution Reversal

Air Pollution Reversal uses Atmospheric Vacuum Cleaner to reverse air pollution. Restorever started the Fight For Our Lungs campaign in Delhi, India on 26 November 2024 to restore air quality in Delhi. Air pollution is severe in Delhi. Delhi's Air Quality Index (AQI) crosses 500 every winter. There is a layer of smog stuck in Delhi skies, visible in flight descent. It is stuck due to lack of wind. Use N95 mask.

Fight For Our Lungs has the following steps:
1. Declare public health emergency
2. Atmospheric Vacuum Cleaner to clear smog
3. Large wind farms generate wind
4. Japanese dust-free demolition methods (Kajima Cut & Take Down Method, Tecorep System)

Sources and health risks:

PM2.5: smoke, stubble burning, firecrackers, vehicle emissions. Health risks are lung inflammation, increased asthma worsening and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) and cardiovascular disease.

PM10: dust from construction. Health risks are throat irritation and respiratory diseases.

Hurricane Defense

Hurricane Defense is a division in Restorever to combat hurricanes (cyclones) by disrupting hurricane formation. Hurricanes are formed over seawater from rising moist air from hot sea surfaces, generating thunderstorms with a low pressure center and low vertical wind shear. Hurricanes require the Coriolis force for formation. Hurricanes are exacerbated by rising sea surface temperatures caused by climate change.

Hurricane Defense is conducting a feasibility study on hurricane disruption using computational fluid dynamics (1). This report will inform further experimental tests with the permission of relevant government agencies. Each hurricane causes $100-120 billion in damages, evident in hurricanes Milton (2024) and Helene (2024) in USA.
