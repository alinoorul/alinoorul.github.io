RESTOREVER


Freshwater shipping

Restorever ships freshwater from melting glaciers to restore dry rivers, power hydroelectric dams and fill lakes. More than 20 billion tons of freshwater available annually.

We have partners in Greenland with licenses to ship 20 billion tons of freshwater annually. Per-liter costs are 5-10x compared to desalination but an unprecedented volume of water is available. This water is suitable for drinking with minimal filtration, agriculture and industrial purposes. Increased availability of water is a silver lining to global warming that we and subsequent generations must use to prosper.
