SELF -REFERENTIAL LAYER


A corpus of questions asked of a bot and answers generated by the bot serves as an

approximation of memory. By labeling the corpus differently from the rest, I want to

bootstrap the concept of self in the bot.

I believe this will help in bootstrapping consciousness in bots. Using this corpus to

retrain is not useful because answers in this corpus are exactly what the agent

generates. But this corpus is useful in 2 contexts I can think of: differentiating the self

from others through communication corpuses and maintaining a memory of self.
