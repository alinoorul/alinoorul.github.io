BIOTECH, AWAITING REVOLUTION


I attended BIO 2023. The business end of biotech seems to think the biotech

revolution has occurred.

It has not.

Biotech is mostly medicine right now. Some parts include agriculture and

limited commodities, but that’s it.

A revolution comes when an industry seeps into lives so tangibly that they’re

changed forever. Wheel, electricity, Internet, iPhone.

The biotech revolution will come when average people experience

biotechnology in first person. From biology beyond medicine.
