ANTARCTIC STOCK SIMULATION


A stock exchange game called Antarctic Stock Simulation. The stock exchange is for mathematical minds to play with. Listing criterion is changing the world and The Matrix's ownership, because listed companies have either been started in or are vetted by The Matrix. Currently listing 18 corporations.

You need a share in ASS to tap it (to play). Antarctic Stock Simulation lists itself. Everyone who wants to buy stock in companies listed on Antarctic Stock Simulation needs to own at least one share of Antarctic Stock Simulation. Think of it as a $1000 fee for access to the multiplayer game. You can play with yourself in the singleplayer game, which is an independent simulation of the game that runs locally on your computer. The fee helps Antarctic Stock Simulation operate the multiplayer version.
