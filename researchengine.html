RESEARCH ENGINE


Get a webdex

Research Engine is an Internet scientific journal that curates the global dex collection.

Get your webdex here. Python required.

Webdex puts your research papers on the Internet. Put PDFs of your research papers in a folder. Webdex makes a website from PDFs with Python. You need to know some Python to operate it.

Run dev.py once. Put PDFs in raw folder. Run dev.py twice. Index.html in the dex folder is your website.

Put this website on GitHub Pages. You should have a text-only collection of pages. Each page is your research paper.

This is your own journal freely available on the Internet.

Put out truth because credibility lost is not won back.

Research Engine will one day be a social network to connect academia. This platform is primarily based on verified .edu emails.
