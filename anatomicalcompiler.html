ANATOMICAL COMPILER


A machine that takes in simple instructions and outputs a specific organism.

I was introduced to this in a podcast with Lex Friedman and Michael Levin. Cool

concept.

I toured Ginkgo Bioworks . Bioworks refer to factories that Ginkgo has built that do

synthetic biology with extreme automation. I believe a biowork designed to use

Xenobots will be able to create ananatomical compiler.

The compiler will stem from a self -learning program that operates a biowork to

design organisms and understands these organisms better. This will transition into

a full -fledged compiler that can build life: organs, tissues, colonies, biochips.
