WHY DO PRESENTATIONS WORK SO MUCH WITH LISTS?


•It’s less to do with lists, and more to do with lists of lists

•A list contains things. It can contain nthings within, that can be mdifferent things.

One can focus on each one of these things sequentially, in the “list” structure. More

in “bulleted points” .

•But yes, back to the original question. Why do presentations work so much with

lists? Cuz a presentation of 10 slides can be distilled into 1 slide (using the question

asked above to determine inclusion). This 1 slide contains some information of

each slide, this list is of subsequent items, lists of greater detail into each item. A

kind of lossy compression that saves time.
